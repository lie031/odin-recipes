AJIACO

Ajiaco is a delicious chicken and potato soup, popular in Bogot√°.

Ingredients:

  • 3 chicken breasts
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 4 cups water
  • 4 small yellow potatoes, peeled and halved
  • 2 ears of corn, cut into thirds
  • 1 cup guascas (a herb, substitute with oregano if unavailable)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 avocados, sliced
  • Capers, for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, combine chicken breasts, chicken broth, and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes.
  2. Remove chicken, shred it, and return it to the pot.
  3. Add potatoes, corn, and guascas. Simmer until potatoes are tender.
  4. Stir in the heavy cream and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with avocado slices and capers.
