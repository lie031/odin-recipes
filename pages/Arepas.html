AREPAS

Arepas are a staple in Colombian cuisine. They're delicious corn cakes that can be filled with various ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups pre-cooked cornmeal (masarepa)
  • 2 1/2 cups warm water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup grated cheese (optional)
  • Butter or oil for cooking

    • Instructions

    1. In a large bowl, combine the masarepa, warm water, and salt. Mix until a smooth dough forms.
    2. Divide the dough into golf ball-sized portions and shape them into discs.
    3. Optional: stuff each arepa with cheese before cooking.
    4. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat with a bit of butter or oil.
    5. Cook the arepas for about 5 minutes on each side, or until they develop a golden crust.
