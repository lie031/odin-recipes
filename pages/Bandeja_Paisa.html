BANDEJA PAISA

Bandeja Paisa is a hearty and iconic Colombian dish known for its abundance of different elements. It's a true comfort food.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked white rice
  • 1 cup cooked red beans
  • 1 cup ground beef (or chorizo)
  • 1 ripe plantain, sliced and fried
  • 2 eggs, fried
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 cup chicharr√≥n (fried pork belly or pork rinds)
  • 1 arepa (corn cake)
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Arrange all the ingredients on a large plate or platter.
  2. Serve with ajiaco (a Colombian salsa) on the side.
